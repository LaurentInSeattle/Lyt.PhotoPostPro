
Standard photo sizes refer to both physical print dimensions and digital pixel resolutions. 
The most universally standard print size is 4x6 inches, which perfectly matches the 3:2 aspect ratio 
of most smartphones and digital cameras.
A comprehensive breakdown of standard print dimensions, recommended digital pixels (at a high-quality 300 DPI), 
and their common use cases:
Print Size (inches)Aspect RatioApprox. Pixels (300 DPI)Common Use Case
2 x 3      3:2 600 x 900Wallet-size photos
4 x 6      3:2 1200 x 1800Photo albums, postcards

5 x 7      3.5:2.5  1500 x 2100Desk displays, greeting cards

8 x 10     5:4      2400 x 3000Professional portraits, larger frames
16 x 20    4:5      4800 x 6000Large prints, posters, canvas art

11 x 14   11:14     3300 x 4200Wall art, balanced landscapes/portraits
